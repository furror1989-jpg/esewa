Editorial Principles

Verification First

We sign up, deposit, play, and withdraw before a rating goes live. Each review includes timestamped screenshots and tracked TXIDs where applicable.

No Pay-for-Score

Partners can’t buy rankings. Listings marked as sponsored are clearly disclosed and evaluated against the same checklist.

User Safety

We highlight KYC, limits, self-exclusion, and dispute paths. If responsible-gaming tooling is weak, we flag it.

Local Relevance

We prioritize NPR handling, local support availability, and wallet behavior specific to Nepal (e.g., eSewa and bank links).

Review Methodology

  • Onboarding: registration UX, KYC clarity, data retention notes.
  • Payments: min/max, fees, currency handling, pending times, reversal rules.
  • Payout Tests: at least two withdrawals on different days; measure queue time and net receipt.
  • Bonus Logic: wagering math, excluded games, payout caps; simulate edge cases.
  • Support: response latency, language coverage, escalation quality.
  • Fairness & Trust: provider mix, license claims, audit trails, complaints review.
  • Mobile: app stability, battery/network tolerance, crash rate.
