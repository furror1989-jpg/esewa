💳 About eSewa Digital Wallet

eSewa is a leading digital wallet and mobile payment platform in Nepal, launched in 2009 by F1Soft International. It enables users to make online and offline payments, transfer funds, pay bills, and conduct various financial transactions. According to recent reports, it serves millions of users in Nepal and is integrated with banks and other services. Regulated by Nepal Rastra Bank, it emphasizes secure, convenient payments within the country.

Online Casinos in Nepal

Despite their illegality, online casinos targeting Nepali players have proliferated, especially since the COVID-19 era, when internet usage has increased. These platforms often operate from offshore jurisdictions and aggressively market themselves, offering games like slots, poker, and sports betting. A 2021 investigation highlighted their growth, noting that regulators like Nepal Rastra Bank were either unaware or indifferent at the time. By 2025, the sector continues to expand informally, with platforms celebrating milestones. However, participation carries risks, including legal penalties, financial losses, and potential fraud.

Using eSewa in Casinos

eSewa is often listed as a payment method for deposits and withdrawals at Nepal-focused online casinos due to its popularity and convenience for transactions in local currency (NPR). Platforms convert NPR into other currencies (such as INR) for games, often through peer-to-peer agents who process transfers discreetly, sometimes using informal systems like Hundi to avoid tracking. This ensures fast 24/7 transactions but contributes to capital outflow from Nepal. Payment gateways like PlusPeDirect facilitate eSewa for gaming merchants, offering compliant processing for high-risk sectors like casinos and forex. For foreigners, land-based options are safer, but online remains a gray area. Always verify the legitimacy of the platform and comply with local laws.
